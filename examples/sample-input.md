# Sample Opportunity Package

## Opportunity

Opportunity ID: QWEN-RFP-2026-001

Opportunity name: Municipal AI Service Desk RFP

## RFP Text

1. The vendor must provide SOC 2 Type II evidence issued within the last 12 months.
2. The proposal shall include a production support plan with a maximum first response SLA of 4 business hours.
3. The vendor is required to submit pricing in the provided spreadsheet template.
4. The solution must support English and Chinese user interactions.
5. Bidders that do not provide a data retention and deletion policy will be considered non-responsive.
6. The proposal deadline is 2026-07-15 at 5:00 PM local time.
7. The vendor should describe prior deployments for public sector customers.

## Approved Evidence Bundle

Evidence A: Security Overview, section 2.1
Our platform completed SOC 2 Type II review for the period ending 2026-03-31.

Evidence B: Product Localization Guide, section 1.4
The assistant supports English and Chinese user interactions with locale-aware prompts and output review.

Evidence C: Support Policy, section 3.2
Enterprise support includes a 4 business hour first response target for priority 2 incidents.

Evidence D: Data Governance Policy, section 5.1
Customer data can be deleted within 30 days after verified request. Retention periods are configured per contract.
